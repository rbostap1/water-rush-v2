How to Play Water Rush

  • Test your knowledge about water care and facts!
  • Answer each true/false question by clicking the correct button.
  • Each correct answer fills the glass with blue water (fresh water) and gives you 10 points.
  • Each incorrect answer fills the glass with green water (dirty water) and subtracts 10 points (score can't go below 0).
  • If you score 60% or higher with blue water (fresh water), you'll win and see a celebration!
  • Click "Reset Game" to play again.
